About Suhan


Suhan Bangera is an aspiring writer from India, authoring over 25 short stories and flash fiction. An ardent computer programmer by profession, Suhan was born on the fourth of August, 2001 and pursuing his Engineering in Information Science from Ramaiah Institute of technology.

A die-hard Stephen King fan, Suhan writes fiction in horror, thriller and suspense genre.

Always fascinated by the wonders of human imagination, Suhan likes to explore and play with human psyche in his works. Often known for his gory and disturbing style, his stories aren't made for ones with a faint heart.

Suhan's short stories have been published in several anthologiies and he aspires to write a bestselling novelist.

Apart from writing stories Suhan spends his time reading, programming and watching horror movies
